Detailed Description

This class is responsible for initiating the transmission of buffered data. It works by registering the transmission task to the scheduler. The Sense service calls scheduleTransmissions(Context) when it starts sensing.

When the transmission task is executed, an Intent is sent to the MsgHandler to empty its buffer.

The transmission frequency is based on the Main#SYNC_RATE preference. When the sync rate is set to the real-time setting, we look at the and Main#SAMPLE_RATE to determine periodic "just in case" transmissions. In case of transmission over 3G we transmit every one hour for energy conservation.
