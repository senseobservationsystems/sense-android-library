float [] nl.sense_os.service.motion.AccelerationFilter.calcLinAcc ( float[]  values)

Calculates the linear acceleration of a raw accelerometer sample. Tries to determine the gravity component by putting the signal through a first-order low-pass filter.

Parameters
valuesArray with accelerometer values for the three axes.
Returns
